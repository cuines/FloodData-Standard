# Contributing to FloodData-Standard

We welcome contributions from hydrological experts, meteorologists, and data scientists from around the world.

## Contribution Process

All proposals must be submitted via a Pull Request and reviewed before being merged into the main standard.

1. **Fork** this repository.
2. **Create a branch** for your contribution.
3. **Make your changes** and ensure they follow the project's style and conventions.
4. **Submit a Pull Request** (PR) describing your changes and linking to any relevant issues.
5. **Participate in the review process** and address feedback as needed.

All PRs will be reviewed by the project maintainers and the broader community. Once approved, they will be merged into the main standard.

Thank you for helping improve global flood data exchange!
